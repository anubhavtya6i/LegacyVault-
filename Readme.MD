# LegacyVault

> Secure Asset Management & Digital Legacy Platform

## Overview

LegacyVault is a secure web-based platform designed to help individuals organize, manage, and protect their financial assets and important information in one centralized location.

Managing investments, real estate, loans, and other valuable assets often becomes challenging due to scattered records, lack of visibility, and the absence of a reliable system for transferring critical information to trusted family members when needed.

LegacyVault addresses this problem by providing a secure digital vault where users can store, track, and manage their assets while maintaining complete control over who can access their information.

The platform also introduces a Digital Legacy System that allows users to nominate authorized individuals who can gain access to asset information under predefined conditions, ensuring that important financial information is never lost.

* Stocks
* Mutual Funds

* Gold
* Silver

* Fixed Deposits
* Loans

In many cases:

* Asset information is scattered across different applications.
* Family members are unaware of existing investments.
* Critical financial records become inaccessible during emergencies.
* There is no centralized platform for managing digital inheritance.

LegacyVault aims to solve these challenges through secure asset management and controlled legacy access.
